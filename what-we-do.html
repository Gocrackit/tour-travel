Western Perspectives

(Tour Duration: 08 Nights & 09 Days)

City Covered: Mumbai - Aurangabad - Udaipur - Ranakpur - Mt Abu - Jodhpur - Mumbai

Day 01/ Arrival Mumbai

Arrive mumbai, meet assist at the airport and transfer to hotel. Night stay in mumbai.

Day 02/ Mumbai - Aurangabad (By Flight)

After breakfast proceed for half day tour of mumbai covering gateway of india – the stone archway designed by wittett in the 16th century gujarati style, prince of whales museum , chowpatty beach, dhobhi ghat, marine drive. Later transfer to the airport to board flight to aurangabad, the capital of aurangzeb, who was the last of the great mughals. Arrive aurangabad and transfer to hotel. Night stay in aurangabad.
